# Deep Dive Scripture Study

Conduct a thorough study of the scripture passage: **$ARGUMENTS**

## Study Process

### 1. Text Analysis
- Quote the full passage text
- Identify key words and phrases
- Note any Hebrew/Greek meanings that illuminate understanding (for OT/NT)
- Identify literary devices (chiasmus, parallelism, metaphor, etc.)

### 2. Historical & Cultural Context
- When was this written and by whom?
- What was happening historically?
- What cultural context helps understanding?
- Where does this fit in the overall narrative?

### 3. Cross-References
Search for and provide related scriptures from:
- Other Old Testament passages
- Book of Mormon connections (especially helpful for Isaiah)
- New Testament fulfillments or echoes
- Doctrine and Covenants/Pearl of Great Price references
- Topical Guide connections

### 4. Prophetic Commentary
Search for what modern prophets and apostles have said about this passage in General Conference or other talks.

### 5. Gospel Principles
- What doctrine or principles does this teach?
- How does this point to Christ?
- What covenants or ordinances does this relate to?

### 6. Personal Application
Provide thoughtful questions for personal reflection:
- How does this apply to my life today?
- What can I learn about God's character?
- What invitation or call to action is here?
- How might I act on this?

### 7. Journal Prompts
Suggest 2-3 specific writing prompts for deeper reflection.

## Output Format

Structure your response with clear headers. After the study, offer to:
1. Save notes to the appropriate file in `notes/old-testament/` or `notes/book-of-mormon/`
2. Explore parallels to Kyle's interests using `/parallels`
3. Find related conference talks using `/conference`
4. Begin a reflective interview using `/interview`
