# Welcome to Scripture Study

This is a collection of personal scripture study notes, insights, and connections from studying the Old Testament (Come Follow Me 2025), the Book of Mormon, and General Conference talks.

## What You'll Find Here

### Old Testament
Deep dive studies into the Pearl of Great Price, Genesis, Exodus, and beyond. Exploring themes of creation, covenant, prophecy, and the coming Messiah.

### New Testament
Essays and reflections on the teachings of Jesus, the parables, and the path of discipleship.

### General Conference
Research and notes on prophetic teachings organized by theme and scripture reference.

### Parallels & Connections
Explorations connecting gospel principles to personal interests like anime, Lord of the Rings, programming, and literature.

### Personal Reflections
Wrestling with questions, working through doctrinal puzzles, and personal applications of gospel truths.

## Come Follow Me 2025

This year's study focuses on the Old Testament. Key themes being explored:
- **Genesis**: Creation, covenants, patriarchs
- **Exodus**: Deliverance, law, tabernacle
- **Pearl of Great Price**: Moses's vision, Abraham's astronomy, divine identity
